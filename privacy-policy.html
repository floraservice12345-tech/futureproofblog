Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Summary: We collect only what's needed to run this blog. We never sell your data. You can unsubscribe or request data deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

FutureProof Blog ("we", "us", "our") is an independent blog at futureproofblog.in that provides AI, career and personal finance insights for Indian professionals. Contact us at: contact@futureproofblog.in

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the following personal data:

  • Name and email address — when you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • IP address and browser information — automatically collected through server logs and analytics
  • Cookies — for analytics and advertising (see Section 6)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Send you the weekly newsletter you subscribed to
  • Send the free guide ("10 AI Tools for Indian Professionals") promised on subscription
  • Understand which articles are most useful (via anonymous analytics)
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)

We never sell, rent, or share your email address or personal data with third parties for marketing.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on consent — you actively subscribe with your email address. You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email and name for as long as your subscription is active. If you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our mailing list within 30 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

6. Cookies and Advertising

FutureProof Blog uses the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function correctly

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@futureproofblog.in
