The Story Behind This Blog

"In early 2025, I sat at my desk staring at a job-automation report that listed my role as 'high risk.' At the same time, my SIP had underperformed for three years running, and every finance influencer I followed was either selling courses or giving advice designed for Americans. I couldn't find one trustworthy, practical, India-specific resource that honestly answered: Is my career safe? Where should I actually put my money? Which AI tools are worth using? So I built one." — Geeta Yadav, Founder

FutureProof Blog was launched in 2026 with one mission: give Indian middle-class professionals the honest, research-backed answers we wish we'd had earlier. Not recycled global content. Not sponsored fluff. Real analysis written for someone earning ₹8–30 lakh a year, living in an Indian city, making real decisions every day.

Our Editorial Standards

📌 Our 3-Step Publishing Promise

Every article goes through: (1) factual research from primary sources, (2) real-world testing or data verification, and (3) a plain-language edit so a busy professional can act on it in under 10 minutes. We never publish clickbait. We never let affiliate relationships influence what we recommend.

All financial content on FutureProof Blog carries a disclaimer reminding readers to consult a SEBI-registered advisor before making investment decisions. We are not SEBI-registered financial advisors — we are researchers and writers who believe in sharing what the data actually says.
